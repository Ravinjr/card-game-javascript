What is Black Jack Game:
Blackjack, also known as 21, is one of the most popular casino card games worldwide. The game is known for its blend of skill and luck, making it a favorite among both casual and professional gamblers. Blackjack is typically played with one or more decks of 52 cards, and the primary goal is to beat the dealer by having a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
